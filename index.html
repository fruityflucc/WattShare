How It Work

WattShare is a production-ready library of stackable energy trading components built in React Native.

  • Step 1

    Sign Up & Connect

    Create an account and connect your energy device to the blockchain. Join a community of local energy traders.

  • Step 2

    Set Your Preferences

    Choose how much energy you want to share or buy. Set your trading preferences and price limits.

  • Step 3

    Start Trading Energy

    Automatically match with buyers and sellers in your area and trade energy seamlessly in real time.

  • Step 4

    Track & Earn

    Monitor your energy flow, track savings or earnings, and contribute to a sustainable future!

What is WattShare?

Explore the future of energy trading with WattShare. Our platform enables peer-to-peer energy trading, allowing you to buy, sell, and share energy directly with others in your community.

  • Real-Time Energy Prices

    Stay up-to-date with real-time energy prices on WattShare. View current rates and make informed decisions about your energy trading and usage

  • Trade Renewable Energy Credits and Surplus Power
